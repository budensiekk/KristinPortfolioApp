ABOUT

Ashley Hohnstein is a 20 year old design student who was born, raised, and currently resides in Wisconsin. While pursuing a degree in Graphic Design and Interactive Media at the University of Wisconsin-Stout, she leads the UW-Stout chapter of AIGA as president for the 2011-2012 year, actively participating in AIGA Minnesota events. She is especially interested in creating a balance of bold colors, strong typography, and intricate concepts in her work. In the future, she hopes to delve even further into hand-drawn typography and more complex illustrations. She would love to work someday in publication design or packaging. In addition to design, Ashley also enjoys photography, parapsychology and miniature dachshunds.
